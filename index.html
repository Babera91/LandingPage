ABOUT

Claudio Mortensen was born in Jundiaí, São Paulo State, Brazil. Today, he is Italian citizen, living and working in Geneva, Switzerland. Claudio spent years developing his photography while also studying Genetics and Molecular Biology. After a career through international biotech companies, Claudio acquired his Master of Fine Arts and embraced his art production, mixing documental style with abstraction, questioning the significance of perceived reality. Education Master Fine Arts - Academy of Art University - San Francisco, California - 2020.

Panamerican School of Art - Design of Photography - 2005.

Getulio Vargas Foundation Business School - MBA - 2003.

São Paulo University - Biochemistry Institute - MS in Biotechnology - 1995
